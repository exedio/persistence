Test Environments

Each build of COPE is subject to extensive automated unit testing on various environments. An environment is the combination of the Database Management System (DBMS), the JDBC driver and the COPE options. The following table lists all environments, the current build (@BUILD_TAG@) was tested on.
@TABLE@
Environment DBMS JDBC driver COPE options
Name Version Name Version
The COPE Console shows your environment along with the list of enviroments, your version of COPE has been tested on.
You may want to try the tests on your own environment, to make sure, that your project's combination of DBMS, JDBC driver and COPE options works correctly. For your convenience, the junit tests of cope are distributed in an extra jar: exedio-cope-test.jar. This jar contains a test suite com.exedio.cope.CopeTest, that you may execute to verify your environment. Please note, that the demoshop already has an ant target copetest for this purpose.
